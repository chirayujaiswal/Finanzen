Financial Goal Setting

What is financial goals?

Financial goals give you a destination: an emergency fund, no credit card debt, retirement savings. And once you know where you want to go, the next step is to map out a plan to get there.

Brainstorm your goals

Short-Term Goals (< 1 Year)
What do you want to achieve this year?
  • Pay off your credit card balance?
  • Take a vacation?
  • Purchase a new computer?
Mid-Term Goals (< 5 Years)
What do you want to achieve in the next five years?
  • Do you need a new car?
  • Are you saving to pay for higher education?
Long-Term Goals (10+ Years)
Think about ten years from now.
  • Pay off your home loan?
  • Saving for retirement?
  • Saving for children's education?
